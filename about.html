About Us

PT Arthaguna Saranapratama, is a company that was established on September 18, 1989. Focusing on the property sector, PT Arthaguna Saranapratama has worked on several property projects including apartments and housing. The parent company of this company is PT BANGUN TJIPTA SARANA which has dozens of subsidiaries engaged in various fields, including construction, property, manufacturing, agro-industry, hotels & resorts.

Since its founding in 1989, PT Arthaguna Saranapratama has experienced the ups and downs of the property business in Indonesia. However, in every obstacle there is always hope and motivation to keep moving forward. And in the end, by the will of God Almighty, PT Arthaguna Saranapratama hopes to be able to move forward confidently to welcome a brighter future.

Geriya Selaras

Geriya Selaras Housing is the 2nd project of PT. Kesuma Agung Selaras which is a premium residence in Bogor district with a direct view of Mount Salak. PT. Kesuma Agung Selaras itself is a professional developer who has long been engaged in the property sector. Geriya Selaras Housing is a housing in the Ciampea Bogor area with a very profitable investment value. We highlight the green nuances, Asri and cool mountain air and are supported by various facilities in the housing so that every resident of Geriya Selaras can always feel healthy & comfortable place to live.
