Jogging Track

Inkamaya offers an exclusive jogging track facility for residents. Specially designed to meet your and your family's exercise needs while enjoying the beautiful natural scenery, this jogging track is the right choice to spend your free time fit and healthy. With easy and convenient access from every corner of the housing complex, you can enjoy this jogging track anytime and anywhere. Not only that, the jogging track at Inkamaya is also supported by adequate security and lighting facilities to ensure your comfort and safety.

Mosque

There is also a public mosque that can be used by all Inkamaya residents. With the mosque, residents can perform their worship comfortably and safely without having to travel far. The mosque in Inkamaya is designed with modern architecture and complete facilities, so that the congregation can worship comfortably and calmly. In addition, the mosque in Inkamaya also often holds religious activities that can strengthen the ties of friendship between Inkamaya residents.

Swimming Pool

Designed in a modern and elegant style, the available swimming pool is suitable for relaxing or exercising with family and friends. In addition, supporting facilities such as showers and changing rooms are also available to make it easier for residents to use the swimming pool. With the complete and modern swimming pool facilities, residents can feel comfortable and entertained while living in Inkamaya.

Children Playground

This facility is specially designed to meet the needs of children to play and explore safely and enjoyably. Located in an easily accessible environment, our playground is equipped with various interesting games, such as swings, slides, and more. With strict security and supervision, you don't have to worry about the safety of your children while playing here.

Clubhouse

The clubhouse at Inkamaya is a premium facility designed to accommodate various social, recreational, and leisure activities for residents. Featuring modern architecture and a welcoming atmosphere, the clubhouse is the perfect place for families and individuals to unwind and connect with the community. Equipped with versatile spaces for events, meetings, and gatherings, it serves as a hub for fostering strong neighborly relationships. Its strategic location within the housing complex makes it easily accessible, while enhanced security and professional management ensure that all residents can use the facility with peace of mind.
